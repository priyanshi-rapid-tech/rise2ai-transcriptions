# Lesson Summary: Vibe Coding with Lovable

### What are we building?
We're exploring **Lovable.dev** by building an **Interview Tracker Kanban**. This app uses a "drag-and-drop" style interface (similar to Trello) to track job applications through different stages like "Applied," "Phone Screen," and "Offer Received." This project highlights Lovable's ability to handle design-heavy interfaces and its unique "Visual Edit" features.

### Quick thoughts before you start
*   Why is a "Kanban" board more effective for tracking 10 job interviews than a simple spreadsheet or a grocery list?
*   How does the ability to "Voice Prompt" an AI speed up the process of building a complex user interface?

### Expert Vocabulary
*   **Lovable.dev:** A state-of-the-art AI builder known for its beautiful design output and its ability to switch between "Simple" (Local Storage) and "Advanced" (Cloud Database) projects.
*   **Kanban Style:** A visual project management system where tasks (or job applications) are moved across columns to show progress.
*   **Visual Edit Mode:** A "point-and-click" editor within Lovable that lets you change colors or styles of specific components without writing a single word of code.
*   **Livable Badge:** A small "Powered by Lovable" label on your site. In the settings, you can check a box to hide this for a more professional look.

### Key Takeaways
1. **Privacy with Local Storage.** By prompting "No database, use local storage," you ensure that your sensitive job data stays in your browser and is never sent to a corporate server.
2. **Dynamic UI Features.** Lovable can build "Smart" apps where you aren't just a user, but a manager. For example, your app can allow you to add your own columns (like "Rejected" or "Accepted") while the app is running.
3. **Voice-Driven Development.** If you're tired of typing, use the microphone icon. You can say, "Hey Lovable, change all the buttons to a soft blue and add a margin at the bottom," and it will execute the command.
4. **The Visual Logic.** The "Visual Edit" tool is perfect for fine-tuning. If the AI didn't get the "Salary Range" box quite right, you can click it and manually adjust the background color with a color picker.

### Reflection Questions
*   How does moving a card from "Technical Interview" to "Offer Received" change the "Vibe" of the user experience?
*   Why should you wait to "Enable Cloud" (Database) until *after* you've finalized your app's layout using Local Storage?

Lovable.dev is where design meets functionality. It's the perfect tool for when you want your app to not only work perfectly but also look like it was designed by a world-class agency.
